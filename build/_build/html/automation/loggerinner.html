Automate logging into websites

Lumen

Lumen has updated their policy to require all users to log in once a month, or their account will be suspended. I have made a simple python script that can be scheduled and will automatically log in, avoiding any chance of being locked out. For more details, see the github page at the link below.

https://github.com/Jamous/Logger_inner
